=== EU vs EXP VARIANTS ===

EU (Local Nationals):
- Designed for local nationals of Cyprus, Denmark, Greece, Malta, Romania
- Home country is covered as a normal area of cover

EXP (Expatriates):
- Designed for people living outside their home country
- Home country visits are covered for up to 180 days per policy year
- Treatment in your home country beyond 180 days is NOT covered
- Applies to all three tiers: Silver EXP, Gold EXP, Platinum EXP

=== GENERAL EXCLUSIONS (ALL PLANS) ===
- Cosmetic or plastic surgery (unless medically necessary after an accident)
- Experimental or unproven treatments
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Treatment arising from war or civil unrest
- HIV/AIDS (specific conditions apply)
- Routine eye tests and corrective lenses (unless added as optional benefit)

=== HOW TO MAKE A CLAIM ===
1. For planned treatment: Request pre-authorisation via Cigna's online portal or helpline
2. For emergency treatment: Seek treatment immediately, then notify Cigna within 48 hours
3. Submit claim form with original invoices and receipts
4. Claims processed within 10 working days
